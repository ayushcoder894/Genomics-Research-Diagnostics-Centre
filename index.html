FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is DNA paternity testing?

Our DNA paternity tests are 99.99% accurate when the alleged father is the biological father, and 100% accurate when he is not.

How long does it take to get results?

Most DNA test results are available within 3-5 business days. Expedited options are available for urgent cases.

Do you offer home sample collection?

Yes, we offer free home sample collection within Karnal and surrounding areas. Additional charges may apply for distant locations.

Are your DNA tests court admissible?

Yes, we offer legal DNA testing that is court admissible. These tests follow strict chain of custody procedures.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All tests and results are 100% confidential. We follow strict privacy protocols to protect your information.

What samples are needed for DNA testing?

We typically use buccal (cheek) swabs which are painless and non-invasive. Other samples like blood or hair can also be used.
